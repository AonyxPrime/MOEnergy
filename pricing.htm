Pricing

At MO Energy, you will find that our prices are cheaper than other power companies. This is because of our heavy utilization of clean energy! It allows us to give the lowest price per kWh anywhere in Missouri.

There are many discounts available to our customers!

  • New Customer Discount: 3% off of monthly bill for first 2 years
  • AutoPay Discount: 5% off of monthly bill
  • Student Discount: 6% off of monthly bill
  • Veteran's Discount: 7% off of monthly bill
  • First Time Homeowner's Discount: 9% off of monthly bill for the first 2 years
