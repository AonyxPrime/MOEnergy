MO Energy

Everyone could use MO Energy!

At MO Energy, we strive to provide clean, afforable, and accessable energy to all of our customers. We prioritize green technology and are always thinking about our impact on the world. Our reseachers and engineers work hard to make sure that we are the first carbon neutral power company in the United States.

Our Customers can rest easy knowing that we are the leading power company when it comes to renewable energy output, with 42% of our power generation coming from carbon neutral sources. We plan to expand that share of power generation from 42% to 55% in 2026, and end the decade with a remarkable carbon-neutral energy percentage of 68%

The Leader in Green Power Generation

MO Energy is ranked #1 among all power companies in the United States in terms of carbon neutral energy production. We are able to to achieve this through our steadfast development and deployment of nuclear reactors and our high efficiency wind farms. We also have plans for the next 30 years, with the closure of all of our fossil fuel power plants occurring in the early 2030's. This is the most ambitious plan proposed by any power company in the United States, and we are the only one to be ahead of their own plans.

Why is MO Energy the right choice?

MO Energy takes great pride in being the leading green energy company. With our investments in renewables and nuclear energy, you and your family can be certain that you are safe and can always rely on MO Energy to serve your power demands.

  • Award Winning Saftey Our power plants have not had an accident since 1989, so you can be sure that we know how to safely produce electricity for everyone.
  • Advancements in Nuclear MO Energy supports the United Stated Department of Energy in their quest for more sustainable nuclear power. Together with the University of Missouri, MO Energy is funding research into thorium and other nuclear reactors that are 100mw or less for small scale application.
  • Our Green Future MO Energy is determined to be completely carbon neutral by 2050, with an already staggering percentage of 42% of our current energy production coming from carbon neutral sources.
  • Amazing Deals MO Energy is the only energy company in the midwest to offer a student discount to all college students! We are also the only company to directly subsidize solar panel installation on your own house!
